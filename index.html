AI‑based triage & diagnosis demo

Lifecare Hospital is exploring AI‑based Clinical Decision Support to assist clinicians in triage and early diagnosis. Experience a safe, educational demo of our Lifecare Assistant.

How the demo works

  1. Answer a short set of questions about age, main symptom, and severity.
  2. The assistant estimates a risk band and possible symptom category.
  3. You receive general guidance on whether an urgent visit is recommended.

The assistant does not store real health data and cannot diagnose or treat. It is designed only to illustrate how AI‑CDSS can support clinicians.

AI inside Lifecare (concept)

  • Machine learning models that score triage priority based on patterns.
  • NLP to interpret free‑text symptom descriptions and notes.
  • Predictive analytics to highlight early risk of deterioration.
  • Explainable alerts so clinicians see why a case is marked high‑risk.

About this project

This Lifecare Hospital website and AI triage demo have been created as part of an academic project on AI‑based Clinical Decision Support Systems for patient triage and diagnosis.

Project structure

The work covers healthcare industry context, the triage‑diagnosis process, and how AI technologies such as machine learning and NLP can improve outcomes.

This website translates that research into an experience similar to leading hospital platforms while emphasising that the AI features here are purely demonstrative.
